Bellabeat Case Study

Data Insights that
changed the strategy.



16.5h
Daily Sedentary Time
Average Fitbit user spends 989 minutes (≈16.5 hours) per day completely sedentary. High-intensity activity is virtually negligible — a massive gap for Bellabeat.
Source · Fitbit dataset · 33 users · 31 days
R²=0.07
Steps ≠ Better Sleep
The popular belief "walk more, sleep better" is statistically flawed. Near-zero correlation proves the real sleep drivers are stress and lifestyle — not steps.
Source · Scatter analysis · Steps vs. Total Hours Asleep
Sunday
The Strategy Gap Day
Activity peaks on Saturday but crashes on Sunday — lowest of the week. This creates a prime re-engagement window for targeted push notifications and ads.
Source · Average Daily Steps by Weekday · 7-day analysis
